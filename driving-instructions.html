Driving Instructions:



  • First, always know what does what in your vehicle. Never get confused with the operation of something with another.
  • Second, Always look in the mirror before changing lanes. Irrespective of a vehicle being behind you, always indicate your intention of changing lanes using indicator.
  • Third, apply sudden breaks only when absolutely neccessary.
  • Fourth, never overspeed.
  • Always drive in the speed limit that your vehicle allows. Otherwise it is difficult to control.
  • Always drive with the thought that the other person might make a mistake to avoid accidents.
  • Never drink and drive.
  • Never drive without license.
  • Never drive without wearing a seatbelt in 4 wheelers and helmet in 2 wheelers.
  • Never use phone while driving.
  • Never park in "No Parking" zone.
